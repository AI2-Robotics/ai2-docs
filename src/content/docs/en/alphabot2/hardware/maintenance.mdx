## Routine Maintenance Items and Intervals

To ensure proper operation of the AlphaBot 2 and extend its service life, perform regular maintenance according to the following schedule.

|Maintenance Item|Description|Recommended Interval|
|:---|:---|:---|
|Appearance Inspection|Check the robot for obvious damage, wear or collision.|Daily|
|Appearance Cleaning|Clean robot parts with clean, dry cloth to maintain a clean appearance and avoid moisture and corrosive cleaners.|Daily|
|Emergency Stop Test|Periodically test the emergency stop function to ensure its reliability.|Daily|
|Fastener Inspection|Check and tighten all screws and fasteners.|Daily|
|Electrical Connection Inspection|Ensure all electrical connections are secure, with no loose contacts or damage.|Monthly|
|Software Update|Check and update the control software and applications.|Whenever there is an update|
|Software Data Backup|Back up critical software configurations and data regularly.|Quarterly|
|Firmware Update|Check and update the firmware regularly.|Whenever there is an update|
|Sensor and Component Inspection|Inspect sensors and other critical components to ensure they work normally.|Monthly|

## Warranty Period

1. The product you have purchased is covered by a one-year warranty from the date of delivery. During the warranty period, if the product experiences performance failures under normal usage conditions not resulting from user damage or misuse, we will provide free repair or parts replacement services.

2. During the warranty period, if the product is modified, disassembled, or altered without our authorization, this warranty will become void in its entirety.

3. If your product is beyond the warranty period, you may still receive technical support and repair services for a fee, charged in accordance with our standard rates. Extended warranty services, if available, must be purchased before the original warranty expires.
