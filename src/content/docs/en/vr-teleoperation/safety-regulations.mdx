## Operating Instructions

- This product must be used properly in accordance with this manual. Improper use may pose operational risks.
- The operating humidity range is 25–80% RH. Do not use the product in environments with humidity exceeding 80% RH.
- The operating temperature range is 0–40°C. Keep the product away from open flames and heat sources; otherwise, damage may occur.
- **Warning: This product is not waterproof.**
- Do not disassemble the product yourself. For repair services, please contact our after-sales team.
- Before powering off the robot, you must first stop all controls started via the HMI. Wait 10 seconds after the HMI interface has closed before shutting down the robot.
