## Unboxing

After receiving the VR Teleoperation Device, follow the steps below to unbox it:

<Steps>

1. Inspect the packaging box to ensure it is intact.
   <Aside>
    If the box is damaged, please contact our company and the logistics company immediately.
   </Aside>

2. Refer to the list below to ensure no parts are missing or damaged.

   <Aside>
   - The following is for reference only; please refer to the actual delivered items.
   - Please keep the packaging box and materials properly for future transportation or maintenance.
   </Aside>

   - **Standard Configuration**: 
      - 1 VR set (1 VR Headset, 1 Left Controller, 1 Right Controller)
      - 1 VR Data Cable
      - 1 Charging Plug
      - 1 2-meter Type-C charging cable
      - 1 8-meter Ethernet cable
      - 1 Industrial PC (with accessories + USB/WiFi)
      - 1 Keyboard & Mouse Set
      - 1 Foot Pedal
      - 1 Monitor
      - 2 AA Batteries
   - **Optional Configuration (if applicable)**:
       - **Dexterous Hand**: 1 Pair of Dexterous Hand Gloves, 2 Data Cables, 1 User Manual.
       - **Video Transmission Module**: 1 Video Transmitter, 2 Disks, 2 Omnidirectional Shock‑Absorbing Antennas, 1 Ethernet Cable, 1 Female Power Connector, 1 3 m Power Adapter.

</Steps>
