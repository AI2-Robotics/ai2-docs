## Encoder Light Does Not Illuminate

* Cause:

    1. Power supply not connected.
    2. Loose wiring.

* Solution:
    
    1. Ensure the exoskeleton teleoperation device is correctly connected to the power supply.
    2. Tighten the connecting wires after powering off, then power on again.

## No Data on AI2-HMI Interface

* Cause:

    1. The USB cable between the exoskeleton teleoperation device and the Industrial PC is not connected or is loose.
    2. The data acquisition unit is not functioning properly.

* Solution:
    
    1. Ensure the exoskeleton teleoperation device is correctly connected to the industrial PC.
    2. Disconnect both the USB cable and the acquisition unit power supply; then reconnect by powering on the acquisition unit first, followed by connecting the USB cable.

## Non-Zero Value on AI2-HMI Interface When Encoder Is at Zero Position

* Cause:

    1. Incorrect zero calibration.
    2. Improper offset configuration.

* Solution:
    
    1. Re-calibrate the encoder zero position.
    2. Modify the corresponding encoder offset value in the configuration file.  


## Other Problems

If you encounter other problems, please first try restarting the device. If the problem still exists, please contact our support team.
